Mahindra Be6

Mahindra BE 6: Bold Design & Next-Gen Tech

The Mahindra BE 6 is a sleek, rear-wheel-drive electric SUV built on the INGLO platform. It offers two battery options: a 59 kWh pack with a 556 km range and a 79 kWh pack delivering up to 682 km on a single charge. With up to 286 bhp, it accelerates from 0–100 km/h in 6.7 seconds. Features include dual 12.3-inch displays, AI-powered MAIA system, fast DC charging, and a 5-star Bharat NCAP safety rating.

Mahindra XUV700

Mahindra XUV700: The SUV with Power and Luxury

Mahindra XUV700 is a feature-rich 5- and 7-seater SUV that offers both petrol and diesel engine options. It delivers up to 197 bhp and comes with a 6-speed manual or automatic transmission. The SUV boasts advanced features like ADAS, a panoramic sunroof, and a 360-degree camera. It is available in multiple variants, with prices ranging from ₹13.45 lakh to ₹25.48 lakh (ex-showroom).

Maruti Suzuki Swift

Maruti Suzuki Swift: Style Meets Performance

The Maruti Suzuki Swift is a popular compact hatchback known for its stylish design and fuel efficiency. It offers a 1.2L petrol engine delivering 80.46 bhp and a mileage of up to 25.75 kmpl. The Swift is available in both manual and automatic transmissions, with prices ranging from ₹5.79 lakh to ₹8.80 lakh (ex-showroom). It comes equipped with features like a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto connectivity.
