PUMA

PUMA is a global sportswear brand headquartered in Germany, founded in 1948 by Rudolf Dassler, which designs, sells, and markets footwear, apparel, and accessories. The company is a multinational corporation specializing in athletic and lifestyle products across categories like football, running, and motorsports, known for blending sports, fashion, and street culture.

VAN HEUSEN

Van Heusen is a premium lifestyle and apparel brand known for its sophisticated styles in men's and women's formal, casual, and ceremonial wear, with a history of innovation dating back to its patented "soft-folding collar" in 1919. The brand, originally an American company, is now owned by Authentic Brands Group and operates in India through Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited (ABFRL), focusing on quality, timeliness, and providing fashion for a wide range of occasions.
